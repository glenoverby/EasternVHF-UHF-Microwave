April 15-18, 2027
Hilton Garden Inn (860)-688-6400
555 Corporate Drive, Windsor, CT 06095


Papers and presentations on all VHF, UHF, and Microwave topics are solicited and welcome.

This is the 50th Anniversary of the Conference - let's make it a special one.

We are nine months away, so there is time to prepare a good presentation or paper. Is there something you've been meaning to investigate or try out, or something you have done and been meaning to write up?

If you are worried about your writing or speaking skills, put your thoughts and some pictures together and let me know, and we will find some help. At least one of our NEWS members does lots of neat stuff but is very shy about speaking so we try to encourage him and pair up to make presentations. Lots of pictures can make a good presentation without a lot of talk.
