DIRECTIONS & RESERVATIONS



The conference rate for Thursday, Friday, and Saturday night is $149 per night. Three options with King beds are available. If you need a double queen bed room, please call the Hotel Directly at 860-688-6400.


Book a hotel room at the conference rate with this link:

Hilton Garden Inn at Bradley Airport
